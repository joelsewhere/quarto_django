In the last 3 months, matriculation projections (generated 60-21 days prior to a cohort start date) have had an average error between -5 and 10. From a probabilistic perspective, the data team’s goal is for modeling forecasts to land between -10 and 10, and we prefer to under-predict rather than over-predict, so at a high level we are quite happy with these results. They do not, however, tell the entire story.
